What is SciBarCamb?

Science Barcamp Cambridge (SciBarCamb) is a gathering of scientists, publishers, technologists, and others with an interest in science. The goal of the event is to create connections between people who have a lot in common, but don't work in the same field and may not meet each other otherwise. Previous SciBarCamp meetings in the US and Canada have attracted researchers, science communicators, entrepreneurs, artists, media professionals, librarians and scientific publishers.

Unlike regular conferences, SciBarCamb does not have a predefined programme. It is an “unconference” - an event where the participants fill the schedule on the opening night, with topics they want to discuss and talks they want to present.
